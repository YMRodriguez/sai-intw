Title: The History of the Olympic Games

Abstract: This paper traces the Olympics from ancient Greece to the modern era.

1. Origins
   The games began in 776 BC in Olympia, Greece.

2. Modern Revival
   Revived in 1896 by Pierre de Coubertin.

3. Conclusion
   The Olympics remain a global cultural event.
