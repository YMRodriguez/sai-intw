Title: Ethical Considerations of CRISPR in Human Gene Editing

Abstract: This paper debates the moral implications of CRISPR in humans.

1. Introduction
   CRISPR can edit human embryos, raising ethical questions.

2. Ethical Issues
   Concerns include designer babies and unintended genetic consequences.

3. Conclusion
   Human applications remain controversial, with no focus on agriculture.

References:
- Doudna, J. A. (2017). CRISPR ethics.
