Title: Economic Benefits of Deep-Sea Mining

Abstract: This analysis highlights the economic potential of deep-sea mineral extraction.

1. Introduction
   The ocean floor holds vast reserves of rare earth metals.

2. Economic Impacts
   Mining could generate billions in revenue for coastal nations, driving technological innovation.

3. Conclusion
   Economic gains are significant, but environmental effects are not covered here.
